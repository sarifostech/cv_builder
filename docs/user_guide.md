# User Guide – CV Creator

## Getting Started

### Sign Up
1. Click **Sign Up** on the homepage.
2. Enter your email, password (8+ characters), and name.
3. Submit. You’ll be logged in automatically.

### Log In / Log Out
- Use **Login** with your credentials.
- Click your avatar in the top-right and select **Log Out** to sign out.

## Build Your CV

### Create a New CV
- From the dashboard, click **Create New CV**.
- Choose a template (ATS-friendly templates by industry).
- Confirm to create a blank CV.

### Edit Sections
The builder supports these sections:
- Personal Info (name, email, phone, location)
- Summary (short professional summary)
- Experience (add multiple jobs)
- Education (degrees, institutions)
- Skills (list of skills)
- Projects (portfolio items)

Use the sidebar to switch sections. For multi-entry sections (Experience, Education, Skills, Projects), use the **+** button to add items. Click **Delete Section** to clear a section.

### AI Tips
- Click the **AI Tips** button in the header to open the tips panel.
- Choose a category: Verbs, Keywords, or Metrics.
- Click a suggestion to insert it into the currently focused text field.
- Tips are tailored to the current section and a default industry (Technology). More industries coming soon.

### Preview
Toggle between **ATS‑Safe** (plain text) and **Visual** (styled) preview using the buttons in the preview pane.

### Autosave
Your work autosaves every 2 seconds. A “Saving…” indicator appears briefly. You can also rename the CV by editing the title in the header.

## Export PDF

### Free Users
- Can export **ATS‑Safe** PDF only.
- Trying to export Visual or Both shows an upgrade prompt.

### Pro Users
- Can export ATS‑Safe, Visual, or Both (combined PDF).
- Exports are instant and download automatically.

To export: click **Export PDF** in the header (or in the Export modal), select the mode, and confirm.

## Pricing & Upgrade

- **Free Plan**: Create and edit CVs; ATS‑Safe export requires upgrade.
- **Pro Plan**: $12/month or $99/year; unlocks all export formats and all templates.
- Visit **/pricing** for details. Upgrade via the upgrade modal (Stripe integration coming soon).

## FAQ

**Q: Can I change my template later?**  
A: Not yet. Choose carefully; template switching is planned for a future update.

**Q: Is my data safe?**  
A: We store CVs in a secure PostgreSQL database. Passwords are hashed. All traffic is over HTTPS.

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**  
A: You can cancel anytime in account settings (billing page). Your Pro access continues until the end of the billing period.

**Q: Why do I need to log in?**  
A: Saving and accessing your CVs from any device requires an account.

**Q: What browsers are supported?**  
A: Latest two versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.
